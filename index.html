How to Play:

You vs AI

  • Click button to roll dice
  • Points are given for the sum of the two dice
  • If a "1" is rolled, points are void for the roll
  • If both dice are the same value, the sum is multiplied by 2
  • Any other combination is just summed up
  • Game consists of 3 rounds (rolls of dice)
  • At the end of 3 rounds, the winner is determined by the total of 3 rounds
  • Enjoy!
